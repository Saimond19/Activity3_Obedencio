About us "The Fort Cafe"


The Fort Cafe was founded by passionate food enthusiasts who transformed a historic building into a warm and inviting café, inspired by the city’s heritage and infused with contemporary flair. A charming and cozy coffee shop nestled in the heart of Cagayan De Oro. We pride ourselves on being more than just a café; we are a destination where people can come together to enjoy great food, exceptional coffee, and warm hospitality. At our café in CDO, we believe that every cup of coffee tells a story. That’s why we carefully source our beans from trusted local suppliers, ensuring that each sip is rich in flavor and character. Our passionate baristas are trained to craft the perfect cup of coffee, using their expertise and creativity to bring out the best in every bean. We take pride in being part of the vibrant Cagayan De Oro community and strive to create an inclusive environment where everyone feels welcome. So come on over, grab your favorite cup of joe, and make yourself at home in our café – we can’t wait to serve you!

Indulge in a culinary experience like no other at The Fort Cafe, where you can savor the best food in Cagayan de Oro. Located in the heart of the city, this hidden gem offers a delightful array of dishes that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more.
